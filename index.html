FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need to replace my existing cameras?

No. ai4cctv works with any standard IP, HD or HD analog camera. We add our AI software layer on top of your existing infrastructure.

How long does setup take?

Most deployments are live within 24 to 72 hours. Our team handles setup, enrolment, and policy configuration.

Is facial data stored securely?

Yes. All biometric data is encrypted using AES-256 and stored either on-premise or in our ISO-certified cloud. We never share data with third parties.

Can I integrate reports with our payroll?

Absolutely. ai4cctv exports reports in Excel and PDF formats compatible with all major payroll and HR software. Custom API integration is also available.

What happens if a camera goes offline?

The dashboard flags offline cameras in real time. Attendance data for that camera is retained and synced once connectivity is restored.
